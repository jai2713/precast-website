01 — Precast Wall Panels

Factory precision.
Site-ready in days.

Our tilt-up and off-site fabricated wall panels are cast in a controlled environment to exact tolerances — eliminating on-site formwork, reducing wet trades, and cutting wall installation time by up to 60%.

  • Tilt-up and off-site fabricated systems
  • Custom dimensions, textures & finishes
  • Structural and architectural panel options
  • NCC-compliant fire & acoustic ratings

02 — Warehouse & Industrial Construction

Built for scale.
Engineered to last 50 years.

Modern logistics demands buildings that carry the load. Our warehouse builds use precast structural systems for maximum clear-span space, faster completion, and proven durability under heavy operational use.

  • High-bay and distribution centre builds
  • Tilt-panel warehouse systems
  • High load-bearing floor slabs
  • Dock, entry and mezzanine integration

03 — Structural Columns & Beams

Precision-engineered.
Structurally certified.

Our precast structural elements — columns, beams and hollowcore slabs — are manufactured to AS 3600 standards and delivered with full certification. Every element arrives dimensionally correct, ready to place.

  • Precast columns, I-beams and T-beams
  • Hollowcore floor and roof planks
  • Prestressed structural elements
  • Engineer-certified shop drawings provided

04 — Architectural Facades

The face of your building.
Built to make a statement.

Precast concrete isn't just structural — it's architectural. We produce custom facade panels with exposed aggregate, board-form, sandblasted and smooth finishes that transform commercial buildings into landmarks.

  • Insulated sandwich panel systems

  • Stain-resistant sealed finishes available
